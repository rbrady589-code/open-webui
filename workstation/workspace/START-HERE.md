# Business Workstation

Open this folder in Computer as `/workspace`.

## Daily Control

- `projects/the-machine`: command center and operating system.
- `projects/eachtra-kb`: business knowledge base.
- `projects/revenue-engine`: revenue workflows.
- `projects/booking-agent`: booking and lead handling.
- `projects/meta-ads-automation`: paid social automation.
- `projects/creative-os`: creative production system.
- `projects/creative-feedback`: creative review and feedback.
- `projects/research-engine`: research workflows.
- `projects/eachtra-website`: public site.
- `projects/restless-audio`: audio work.

## Platform

- `projects/platform/open-webui`: your Open WebUI fork with the workstation bundle.
- `projects/platform/computer`: Computer fork, the remote workstation layer.
- `projects/platform/oikb`: knowledge-base sync.
- `projects/platform/mcpo`: MCP/OpenAPI bridge.
- `projects/platform/open-terminal`: fallback terminal environment.

## Working Folders

- `inbox`: drop files here from any device.
- `notes`: plans, scratch notes, decisions.
- `memory`: durable context exports and memory snapshots.
- `automation`: recurring task definitions and run notes.
- `outputs`: deliverables.

## Safety Rule

Do not put raw credentials in this workspace. Keep secrets in local environment files, password manager entries, or a dedicated vault.
